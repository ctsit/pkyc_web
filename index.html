Coronavirus Research Testing @ PKY!

Thank you for participating in the Coronavirus Research Testing at P.K. Yonge school!

FOLLOW-UP PARTICIPATION

We are looking forward to the next round of testing and hope that you will continue to participate. For your child’s next round of testing, please expect a similar experience with both a throat swab and a fingerstick. Given expanded testing demands, please note that the swab result will be returned in 4-5 business days after testing and the finger result will take no less than 8 weeks to result.

Individual links to schedule your child’s next appointment were sent to you by email. Only those who consented to participation in antibody testing and associated follow-up are eligible. Contact the study team at pkcovid19@epi.ufl.edu or 352-328-0615 if you have questions.

EXPANDED TESTING OF HOUSEHOLD CONTACTS

In an effort to better contribute to the ongoing, global research on COVID-19, we are expanding our study to include testing of household contacts of the child participating in the original COVID-19 screening study. This addition will help us to better understand the role of children in household transmission dynamics. There is still a great deal that we do not know about COVID-19, and participation in this study of household contacts (e.g. family members or others living with the participating child/ren) will be of great value as we try to protect people from this virus.

What to expect?

If you and/or other adult or child members of your household are willing to participate, we will ask you to complete a short online survey (it should take less than five minutes to complete) and join the student participating from your household to be tested, at the same appointment time. We will test you for active infection (throat swab) and historic exposure to (finger prick blood test) COVID-19. Your information will be linked to your household. We will notify you of positive swab test results by phone and negative swab test results by email. All validated antibody test results will be returned by email no less than 8 weeks after participation.

An informed consent form and survey is required for every household contact that will be participating in the study. If you have more than one child already participating in the study, you only need to add household contacts once; you will have the opportunity to indicate which other children are part of your household in the survey.

When and where?

  • • Friday and Saturday, June 5 and 6 only
  • • Come as a family/household to be tested together
  • • PK Yonge Campus, Front Circle

Who is eligible?

  • • Any household member of an enrolled P.K. Yonge student participant
